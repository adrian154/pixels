Welcome to Pixels, a place for building collaborative pixel art!

Controls: Hold down your mouse to drag the canvas, and use the scroll wheel to zoom in or out. Click to place a pixel. You can use the palette at the bottom of the screen to change colors.

If you want to embed this canvas elsewhere, there's an image version which periodically updates. Pixels is also open source.

But first, please confirm that you're not a robot.
